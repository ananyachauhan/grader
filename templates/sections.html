BUSN 403 - Business Writing and Communication

This is a AI Grader tool that helps professors and teaching assistants grade writing assignments faster and more consistently. It connects with Google Drive to process student submissions, uses AI to evaluate them based on a rubric, and generates feedback showing strengths, issues, and suggestions for improvement. Instructors review and approve results before finalizing grades. The platform also provides summaries and analytics that show overall class performance, helping instructors quickly understand trends, common mistakes, and how students performed as a group.
